Subjects Offered

There are many reasons why a school should offer a range of subjects.
First, it allows students to explore their interests and find what
they're passionate about.
Second, it exposes students to new ideas and perspectives,
which can broaden their horizons and make them more well-rounded individuals.
Third, it provides students with a variety of career options and prepares them for a diverse and ever-changing world. Finally, it helps foster a sense of community by bringing together students with different interests and backgrounds.

More About Curriculum

A curriculum is a set of courses, learning experiences, and materials
designed to meet the educational needs of a student or group of students.
It typically includes the content to be taught,the methods and strategies for teaching it,
and the assessments used to measure student progress.
Curriculum can vary widely, depending on the age of the students, the subject matter,
